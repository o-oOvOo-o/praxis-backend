**Controls:**
- **WASD / Arrow keys** — Move aircraft (left/right/up/down)
- **Space** — Shoot forward
- **Enter** — Start game / Restart after game over

**What's in the game:**
- Third-person blocky aircraft flying over procedurally generated voxel terrain (grass, dirt, stone, water chunks using instanced meshes for performance)
- Billboard/canvas-drawn tanks on the ground that aim and fire red bullets at you
- Yellow player bullets with collision detection against tanks
- Voxel particle explosions on hits
- HUD with health bar, score, distance meter, and heart-based lives display
- Start screen with orbiting camera, game over screen with final score
- Difficulty ramps over time: more tanks, faster fire, increased speed
- Object pooling caps (80 player bullets, 60 enemy bullets, 120 particles, 24 tanks) to keep frame rate stable

Open **http://localhost:8080** in your browser, press Enter, and start shooting tanks.
